Verity Verification

The browser computes SHA-256 for your photo and looks up matching registration records. Token and QR link lookups are also supported.

◇
Verify with a Photo

If the file bytes are identical to the original, SHA-256 will match exactly. The browser computes the hashes locally and only asks the server whether a record exists. Re-encoding or re-saving can prevent a 100% pHash match and a SHA-256 exact match, and a pHash score around 90% can still be considered trustworthy.
